Nathan Hamblen

Nathan Hamblen is founder of a number of Scala free software projects including Dispatch, giter8, and the Scala release announcement site notes.implicit.ly. He is a co-founder of the Unfiltered toolkit for servicing HTTP requests in Scala, co-organizer of the ny-scala Meetup, and Director of Engineering at Meetup.
