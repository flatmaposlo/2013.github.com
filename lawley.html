Ross Lawley

Ross is a Python and Scala Engineer based in London for 10gen - the company behing MongoDB. With over 10 years experience developing web applications Ross holds a deep passion for agile web development. Previously, Ross was the Software Architect for Streetlife and prior to that Ross was the lead developer for Europes largest commercial radio station owner - Global Radio Ltd.
